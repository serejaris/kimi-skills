# Web-Novel (网文) Style

Serialized online fiction for platforms like 番茄/起点. Read [fiction.md](./fiction.md) too — web-novels are fiction with platform-driven pacing. The genre lives or dies on **hooks and momentum**.

## Voice and Tone
- Fast, propulsive, emotionally direct. Strong feelings stated and shown; the reader should feel the 爽点 (satisfaction beats) land.
- Usually third-person limited on the protagonist (first person also common); past or present per the book's convention — stay consistent.
- Conversational, accessible diction (大白话). Vivid over literary; clarity over ornament. Match the subgenre's register (历史权谋 reads weightier than 都市爽文).

## Structure & Format
- **Chapters are short and self-contained**, typically ~2000–4000 字, each a continuous unit — never split one chapter into mechanical "1/2/3" segments.
- **Open chapter 1 with a strong hook in the first few lines** — a reversal, a threat, a striking situation. No slow worldbuilding preamble; weave setting in through action (随风潜入).
- **Every chapter ends on a hook**: cliffhanger, reversal, reveal, or a decision pending. Momentum across chapters is the product.
- **爽点 cadence**: deliver setbacks-then-payoffs on a regular beat (打脸、逆袭、扮猪吃虎、升级). Build tension, then release it; don't stall.
- Foreshadow early, pay off visibly. Keep the protagonist's goal and progress legible to the reader.

## Language Patterns
- Prefer concrete action and consequence: "他一脚踹翻了桌子，账本散了一地" over "他十分愤怒".
- Short punchy lines for impact beats; let key reversals land on their own line.
- Dialogue drives pace — keep it sharp; trim narration around it.
- Subgenre conventions matter: 系统流 (system prompts/面板), 穿越/重生 (前世记忆 as leverage), 历史 (period-appropriate detail and restraint).

## Prohibited Patterns
- Slow openings (weather, genealogy, exposition dumps) — fatal for retention.
- Chapters that end flat with no hook.
- Mechanically uniform paragraphs and the 四字格律 pile (心潮澎湃/热血沸腾/百感交集).
- Protagonist with no clear want, or 爽点 that never arrive.
- AI 腔 (综上所述/值得注意的是/助力/彰显) — instant immersion-breaker.
